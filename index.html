Best Smartphones

22 Oct 2017

Choosing a smartphone can be hard. So, we give you our pick of the best smartphones for the money to help you make a purchase decision.

10.or E (Rs. 7,999)

The 10.or E is a well balanced budget Android phone. It ticks almost all the right boxes; Full HD display with Gorilla Glass protection, a large 4000 mAH battery, stock android, a decent entry-level Snapdragon 430 SoC, and the full suite of sensors including compass and fingerprint scanner. The camera is average at best, but that's to be expected in this price bracket.

There's always something to quibble about, however. The power button in the 10.or E is placed above the volume rockers, which makes it a bit hard to reach for those with small hands. If you're looking for a compact phone, the Redmi 4 is a good option, though it comes with the heavily skinned MIUI.
